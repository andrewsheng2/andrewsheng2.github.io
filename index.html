About Me

I am currently residing in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania and am a current second-year computer science student studying at Carnegie Mellon University, but I am from Brooklyn, New York. I am primarily focused on artificial intelligence and machine learning, and am planning on pursuing a machine learning concentration and physics. I primarily code in Python, C, and SML/NJ but am interested in other languages such as React.js.

I am also passionate about audio engineering, and am currently a part of AB Tech, an organization that provides sound and lighting support to many events. I am primarily a sound engineer, but am also interested in lighting design, and all things Tech.

Here are some of the work and projects that I have done:

Pacman AI Agents

Throughout my Artificial Intelligence course (15-281), I have programmed many different algorithms to solve games of Pacman. These include algorithms such as A*, Integer and Linear Programming, Logical Planning, Reinforcement Learning, as well as making heuristics to make these some of these algorithms more efficient.

Super Py-gon

As a term project in my programming class (15-112), I developed a game based on the popular mobile game Super Hexagon. In addition to recreating the original game in Python, I added many more features to make a more enjoyable experience. These include multiplayer so people can compete against each other, as well as the option to dynamically create obstacles to the beat of a user uploaded song.
